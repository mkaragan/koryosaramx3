Cabbegeche

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cabbage
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp Gochugaru
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1/4 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tbsp of vegetable oil
Preparation
Thinly slice cabbage and mince garlic. Transfer the cabbage to a large bowl and add salt. Let the cabbage rest for 30 minutes and squeeze all the water out. Add garlic, oil, vinegar, coriander powder, gochugaru and salt and pepper to taste.

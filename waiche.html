Waiche

Ingredients

  • 2 cucumbers
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp gochugaru
  • 1/4 tsp coriander powder
Preparation
Rinse and thinly slice cucumbers into half circles. Add to a large bowl with salt and massage until fully coated. Cover the bowl and let it rest for 1 hour and drain excess water. Add soy sauce, gochugaru, coriander powder and salt and pepper to taste.

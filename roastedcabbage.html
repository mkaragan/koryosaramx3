Fried Cabbage

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cabbage
  • 1/2 Onion
  • 1/2 red pepper
  • 1 Roman Tomato
  • 4 cloves of garlic
Preparation
Thinly slice cabbage. Add cabbage to the bowl and add salt to taste. Cover the bowl and leave in the fridge to cool for 1 hour. Squeeze the water of the cabbage. Thinly slice 1/2 an onion and 1/2 red pepper. Dice 1 tomato and mince 4 cloves of garlic. Add oil to medium-sized frying pan and brown the onions over high heat until golden brown. Add peppers and cook until tender. Add tomatoes and garlic. Cook until fragrant and add cabbage. Cook for 5 minutes over medium heat.

Eggs

Ingredients

  • 8 eggs
Preparation
Crack 8 eggs into a bowl and mix. In a small pan, add a little bit of oil. Spread the eggs into a thin layer (should resemble a crepe) Cook on medium-low heat until eggs are fully cooked. Repeat until there is no more egg mixture. Stack the egg sheets, roll them and cut them into thin slices.

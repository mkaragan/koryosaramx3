Kuksi

Kuski is a cold noodle dish often served during celebrations and gatherings. It consist of a refreshing broth, thin round noodles and topped with various salads and meats. Since cooking Kuksi is a process that requires time and attention to detail, it's an ideal activity to share with family and friends, allowing everyone to contribute, bond, and savor the anticipation together.

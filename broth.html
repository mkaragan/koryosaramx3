Broth

Ingredients

  • Roman tomato
  • 1/2 English cucumber
  • 30g of dill (seprate leaf from stem)
  • 10 cups of water
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 4 tsp spoon vinegar
  • 1 tsp splenda
Preparation
Add 10 cups of water, 1/3 cup of soy sauce, 2tsp salt, 1tsp of Splenda and 4 tsp of vinegar into a large saucepan Dice 1 Roman tomato, cut half a cucumber into thin slices and finely chop dill. Add into the saucepan.

Fried Peppers

Ingredients

  • 1-3 green hot green pepper
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tomato
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 1 can of peeled tomatoes
Preparation
Cut green peppers, 1 red pepper and 1 onion into long thin slices. Dice 1 tomato and mince 6 cloves of garlic. Remove peeled tomatoes from the can. Add oil to a frying pan and cook the onions until golden brown. Add red pepper, cook until tender then add green pepper. Cook for 3 minutes, then add the diced tomatoes and canned peeled tomatoes. Add minced garlic and salt and pepper to taste.

Finish and Serve

Ingredients

  • Fine round noodles
  • 1 cup Vegetable oil
  • 4 tbsp Gochugaru
  • Coriander
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
Preparation
Add water to the saucepan, once the water is boiling, add noodles. Cook for 3 minutes, stirring occasionally. Strain the noodles in a colander and rinse immediately with cold water. Add 1 cup of oil to a small frying pan on medium heat. When hot, add gochugaru to the oil and immediately remove from heat. Add noodles to your broth and garnish with all the salads. Add the gochugaru oil, vinegar, soy sauce to taste and garnish with coriander.
